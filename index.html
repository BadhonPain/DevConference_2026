Frequently Asked Questions

Is DevConf 2026 in-person, virtual, or both?

DevConf 2026 is a fully in-person event. Digital recordings are included with every ticket tier so you can catch sessions you miss.

Can I get a refund if I can't attend?

Tickets are refundable up to 30 days before the event. After that, you can transfer your ticket to a colleague at no extra cost.

Is there a student or academic discount?

Yes. Students and academic staff get 40% off the Standard ticket with a valid .edu address.

What's included in the Team ticket?

Everything in the Pro tier for up to 10 attendees, plus a private lounge, a dedicated recruitment booth, and priority support.

Do I need prior experience to attend?

No. Talks span beginner to advanced tracks, and workshops are labeled by skill level so you can pick what fits you.
